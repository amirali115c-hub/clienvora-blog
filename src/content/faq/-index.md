  - title: What does Clienvora actually do?
    answer: Clienvora is a B2B SEO and copywriting studio. The main site at [clienvora.com](https://www.clienvora.com) covers the full service scope.

  - title: What is GEO and why does it matter for B2B?
    answer: GEO, or Generative Engine Optimization, is the practice of making your content quotable by AI answer engines like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Google AI Overviews. In Seer Interactive's 2025 research, brands cited inside AI Overviews earned 35 percent more organic clicks and 91 percent more paid clicks than those left out. For B2B, it is the largest free distribution channel most companies have not touched.

  - title: Is free SEO still viable in 2026?
    answer: Yes, but not the version most teams run. Publishing more volume into a mixed-quality archive actively hurts rankings now. The free stack that works relies on entity authority, indexing speed, and GEO-ready structure. We cover the specifics in our post [Free SEO Is Not Dead](/blog/free-seo-is-not-dead).

  - title: How is a free audit different from a sales call?
    answer: It is a diagnostic, not a pitch. We read your current organic health, identify the three highest-leverage fixes, and tell you exactly what each will cost to implement. You can take that and execute it yourself, or bring us in. Either way you leave with a prioritized action sequence.

  - title: When does it make sense to bring in professional help?
    answer: The tipping point is usually a site with 30 or more posts, a measurable Search Console baseline, and two or more consecutive quarters of flat or declining traffic despite ongoing publishing. At that stage the problem is structural, not a lack of content, and a prioritized remediation sequence beats another checklist.

  - title: What makes a B2B blog worth reading?
    answer: A useful B2B blog publishes research-backed guidance tied to real outcomes, not filler to hit a posting calendar. The posts here lead with citable data, name the entity clearly, and point to the next action. That is what earns rankings and AI citations instead of sitting in an archive nobody finds.
