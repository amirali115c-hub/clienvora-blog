#### Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through the contact and newsletter forms on the Site, such as your name, email address, and the message you send. We also collect standard server and analytics data, including pages viewed, referring source, and approximate location derived from your IP address.

We use the information you provide to respond to your enquiry, send the newsletter if you subscribed, and improve the content on the Site. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Form submissions on the Site may be processed by a third-party service (such as Formspree) that stores the data on our behalf. Their handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policy. By submitting a form, you consent to this processing.

If you subscribe to the Clienvora newsletter, your email address is processed by our email provider (Buttondown) to send you the dispatch you requested. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in each email. We do not sell or share your email address with other parties.

#### Cookies and Analytics

The Site may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic. AI search engine crawlers (including GPTBot, ClaudeBot, and PerplexityBot) may access publicly published content on the Site as part of normal web indexing.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided to us. To make a request, use the [contact page](/contact).
